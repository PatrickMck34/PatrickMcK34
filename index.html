About Me


I have always found computers fascinating ever since I was around the age of 12 when I started trying to teach myself visual basic coding just so I could make a tile in a game do something neat. From there my path took me down quite a few roads from Electricity to executive chef and even home renovations but all the while I never lost my interest in tech. In April of last Year I was given some shocking news that I was diagnosed with Multiple Sclerosis.

This meant I could not continue the physical labor type jobs that I had been doing until that point. So I started researching and by June of the same year just two months after my diagnosis I was starting at App Academy, the top coding bootcamp in the country. It was challenging, fast paced and extremely difficult but I was determined to finally start the career my 12 yr old self already knew was the one for me.

am hardworking, goal oriented and always put everything I have into finishing what I set my mind to. I am excited to finally say I am a software engineer and I am ready to put the same drive and determination that pushed me through my time at app academy regardless of circumstances, to work for your company.
